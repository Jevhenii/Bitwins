General

What is BitWins?

BitWins is a global Ethereum-based lottery that allows players from all over the world to win Ethereum.

What cryptocurrencies can I use to play?

BitWins only works with Ethereum (ETH). You do not need a special token (altcoin) to play.

What percentage of entry fees go to the winner(s)?

We allocate eighty percent (80%) of all entry fees to winners. Traditional lotteries only allocate fifty percent (50%).

Is there a limit to how many entries I can buy?

No, we do not have a limit. You may play as often as you like.
